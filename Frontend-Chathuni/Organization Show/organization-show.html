    AIESEC in Colombo Central

    AIESEC is a global platform for young people to explore and develop their leadership potential. We are a non-partisan, independent, not-for-profit organisation run by students and recent graduates of institutions of higher education. Its members are interested in world issues, leadership and management. AIESEC does not discriminate on the basis of gender, sexual orientation, disabilities, creed, religion, nor on the basis of national, ethnic or social origin.
